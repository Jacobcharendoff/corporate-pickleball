Frequently asked

The questions every
event coordinator emails first.

What's actually included in the price?

Court rental, professional referees, dedicated producer, full catering (per the format), photography (stills + recap reel), branded scorecards, prizes, on-site emcee, hospitality lead, setup, breakdown, and the post-event recap delivered the next morning. There are zero per-guest fees, ever.

How far in advance should we book?

Off-Sites: 4–6 weeks ideal, 2 weeks possible. Championships: 8–12 weeks ideal. The Toronto Open: applications close June 30 of the prior year. May–September fills first; October has the best availability and arguably the best Toronto weather.

Can people who've never played pickleball play?

Yes — and this is the actual point. Every event includes a 20-minute warm-up clinic with our pros. By minute thirty, your VP of Eng and your Q3 intern are competing on equal footing. Most teams find the new players are some of the strongest by the final bracket.

Do you handle billing and procurement paperwork?

Yes. We're set up as a vendor with most major Bay Street firms already (RBC, TD, BMO, Manulife, McCarthy Tétrault, Bennett Jones). Net-30 terms standard. We handle COI, W-9s, supplier-onboarding paperwork — all the things your finance team will ask for.

What if it rains? What if someone gets injured?

All four venues are indoor — rain doesn't apply. We carry $5M commercial liability insurance per event. Two of our producers are certified first-aid responders. We've never had a non-trivial injury across 147 events.

Can we white-label / co-brand the event?

Always. Court-side signage, branded paddles and scorecards, custom polos, branded recap reel, even a one-off trophy if you want. We send a co-branding spec sheet on the discovery call.
