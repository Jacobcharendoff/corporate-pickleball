Why pickleball, not golf

Golf was a gatekeeper sport.
Pickleball isn't.

Every metric your event coordinator cares about — time, cost, inclusion, memorability — moves the right direction the moment you swap the tee box for the kitchen line.

01 / Time

Done in a half-day.

A foursome at Glen Abbey takes six hours plus an hour each way. A Corporate Pickleball off-site is door-to-door in three and a half.

6.5 hrs→3.5 hrs
02 / Cost

~5× more efficient.

Average per-head spend on a 32-person golf outing in the GTA: $880. The Off-Site, white-glove, catered, photographed: $150.

$28,160→$4,800
03 / Inclusion

Everyone can play.

Pickleball flattens skill differences in a single afternoon. Your VP of Eng and your Q3 intern end up on the same court. That's the point.

Insiders→Whole team

Stories, not slides.

How it works

Tell us the date.
We do the other 41 things.

Every event ships with a dedicated producer, two referees, a courtside emcee, a photographer, and a hospitality lead. You do not lift a finger.

01 / Brief

Tell us the date.

One 20-minute call with our producer. We confirm the venue, the format, the catering, and the budget in writing within 24 hours.

02 / Build

We build around your brand.

Court signage, scorecards, the after-event recap reel, even the trophy. Your logo, our colourway. Production-grade.

03 / Play

You arrive. We run the day.

Pros referee. The emcee narrates. The photographer captures the moments. The kitchen serves at the right time. Your only job is to play.

04 / Share

The recap lands at 9am.

A 90-second hero reel, 60 hi-res stills, a printed leaderboard, and a private Vimeo link. In your inbox the morning after.
